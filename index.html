FYRE 5480

FRC team based in Reno, Nevada

We are FYRE (FIRST Young Robotics Engineers), team #5480, a FIRST FRC robotics team based in Reno, Nevada.

Formed in 2013 with our rookie year in 2015, FYRE is a community high school team with members from public and private schools in the Reno Area, such as Davidson Academy, Galena High School, Reno High School, Bishop Manogue Catholic High School, and homeschooling.

Liesma

(2017 season)

Built to complete the Steamworks challenge, Leah is small and light for maximum speed and maneuverability. She has a four-wheel drive system with two omni-wheels for turning.

Aurora

(2018 season)

Built to complete the Power Up challenge, Aurora has a hexagonal chassis in the shape of a rectangle and trapezoid put together to afford enough space for everything but keep her small for maneuvering and fitting easily on the platform out of other robot’s ways.

Sol

(2019 season)

Built to complete the Destination: Deep Space challenge, Sol is our sturdiest robot with a combination of tried-and-true and new features we haven’t used before.
