FYRE 5480

FRC team based in Reno, Nevada

We are FYRE (FIRST Young Robotics Engineers), team #5480, a FIRST FRC robotics team based in Reno, Nevada.

Formed in 2013 with our rookie year in 2015, FYRE is a community high school team with members from public and private schools in the Reno Area, such as Davidson Academy, Galena High School, Reno High School, Bishop Manogue Catholic High School, and homeschooling.
